Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At Ngaoyi Cleaning, we strive to provide exceptional service. This Refund Policy outlines our procedures for cancellations, refunds, and service guarantees.

Important: All refund requests must be submitted in writing to ngaoyicleaning@gmail.com within the specified timeframes.

1. Cancellation Refunds

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Conditions
More than 48 hours before service 100% Refund No questions asked
24-48 hours before service 50% Refund Administration fee applies
Less than 24 hours before service No Refund Staff already scheduled
No-show No Refund Full charge applies

2. Service Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our work. If you're not satisfied with our cleaning service:

  • Notify us within 24 hours of service completion
  • We will return to re-clean the unsatisfactory areas at no additional cost
  • If a re-clean doesn't resolve the issue, we will provide a partial refund based on the specific circumstances
  • This guarantee does not cover:
    • Pre-existing conditions or damage
    • Areas not included in the service scope
    • Issues reported more than 24 hours after service

3. Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days of approval
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Bank transfer refunds may take 3-5 additional business days to appear in your account
  • We are not responsible for bank fees or currency conversion charges

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind after service has been rendered
  • Failure to provide access to the property at the scheduled time
  • Inability to complete service due to customer-provided incorrect information
  • Services rendered during emergency call-outs where staff were dispatched
  • Cleaning products or supplies purchased specifically for your job

5. Recurring Plan Refunds

For recurring cleaning plans:

  • You may cancel your recurring plan at any time
  • Refunds for unused prepaid sessions will be prorated
  • A 10% administration fee applies to recurring plan cancellations
  • Discounts applied to recurring plans will be recalculated for refund amounts

6. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (acts of God, natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.):

  • We will make every effort to reschedule your service
  • If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be provided
  • Force majeure situations are determined at our discretion

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at ngaoyicleaning@gmail.com with:

  • Your booking reference number
  • Date of service
  • Reason for refund request
  • Any supporting photos or documentation

We will respond to your request within 2 business days.
