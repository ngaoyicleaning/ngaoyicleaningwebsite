About Ngaoyi Cleaning

Your trusted cleaning partner in Gauteng

Our Story

Ngaoyi Cleaning was founded with a simple mission: to make professional cleaning services accessible, reliable, and affordable for everyone in Gauteng.

We started as a small team of dedicated cleaners and have grown into a trusted service provider connecting vetted cleaner teams with homes and businesses across the province.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with our clients.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional cleaning services that enhance our clients' quality of life, delivered by trained professionals who take pride in their work.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted cleaning service provider in Gauteng, known for reliability, quality, and customer-centric service.
